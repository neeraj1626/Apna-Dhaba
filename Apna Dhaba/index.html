Our Story

A Highway
Dhaba Heart
in Navghar

Apna Dhaba was born from a simple belief — that the most honest and soulful food comes from the traditional highway dhabas of Punjab. We brought that spirit to Navghar, Maharashtra, adding a warm and welcoming touch that makes every guest feel at home. Every pot simmers slowly on the flame, every roti is freshly rolled on the tawa, and every dish carries the flavors of tradition. At Apna Dhaba, we don’t just serve food — we serve the timeless taste of authentic Indian dhaba cuisine.

Every pot simmers on slow fire. Every roti rolls fresh from the tawa. Every dish tells a story of generations.
